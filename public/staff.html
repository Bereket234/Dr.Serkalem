Dr. Adera Abdela
Founder and Managing Director of SAK

Dr. Adera Abdela is a founder and Managing Director of SAK. Dr. Adera graduated with BA in Economics and completed his MA and Doctoral studies in Transformational Leadership with distinction from UK and USA respectively. Dr. Adera is an internationally certified Business and Leadership Advisor, trainer and management consultant with key expertise in areas of leadership, management, economics and organizational capacity building. He has led and advised various projects and managed several training and consultancy activities as team leader for different local and international organizations.

Dawit (David) Moges
Economist

A graduate of Fergusson College with a B.A and M.A in economics and MPH from Haramaya University and a doctoral candidate at BGU (USA). Currently, He serves as President of the Ethiopian Employers’ Federation (EEF), 1st Vice President of the Confederation of Ethiopian Employers’ Federation (CEEF). At a regional level, he assumes a position as Secretary-General of the Confederation of IGAD Employers’ (CIE).

Furthermore, he is an experienced Chief Executive Officer with a demonstrated history of working in the manufacturing, hospital & health care industry and Logistic sector. Skilled in Nonprofit Organizations, Program Evaluation, Health Promotion, and Management. He has lectured for four years at Addis Abeba University, trained entrepreneurship at different consultation institutes.
